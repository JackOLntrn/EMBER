## Board Componenets

### x. 

### x. DRV8871DDAR - Motor Driver x2
Unipolar Motor Driver NMOS PWM 8-SO PowerPad - Texas Instruments: [DRV8871DDAR](https://www.digikey.com/en/products/detail/texas-instruments/DRV8871DDAR/5639726)\

### x. 1N4004 - Flyback Diode x1
Diode 400 V 1A Through Hole DO-204AC (DO-41) - 	
Vishay General Semiconductor : [1N4004-E3/54](https://www.digikey.com/en/products/detail/diotec-semiconductor/1N4004/13164524)

### x. MOSFET N-CH x1
N-Channel 60 V 22A (Tc) 3.3W (Ta), 43W (Tc) Surface Mount DPAK-3

### x. 2 Position Screw Terminal x2
2 Position Wire to Board Terminal Block Horizontal with Board 0.100" (2.54mm) Through Hole - Phoenix Contact: [1725656](https://www.digikey.com/en/products/detail/phoenix-contact/1725656/267462)

### x. 4 Position Screw Terminal x3
4 Position Wire to Board Terminal Block Horizontal with Board 0.100" (2.54mm) Through Hole - Phoenix Contact: [1725672](https://www.digikey.com/en/products/detail/phoenix-contact/1725672/267464)

### x. 8 Position Screw Terminal x1
8 Position Wire to Board Terminal Block Horizontal with Board 0.100" (2.54mm) Through Hole - Phoenix Contact: [1725711](https://www.digikey.com/en/products/detail/phoenix-contact/1725711/267468)
